<p><p>Garment printers market can be categorized into two main types: flat screen printers and rotary screen printers. Flat screen printers use a flat or horizontal screen to transfer ink onto fabric, making them suitable for simple and small-scale designs. On the other hand, rotary screen printers use a cylindrical screen to print continuous patterns on fabric, making them ideal for large-scale and intricate designs. Both types of printers have their own advantages and are commonly used in the textile and garment industry.</p></p>
