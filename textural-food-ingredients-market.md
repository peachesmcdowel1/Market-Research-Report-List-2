<p><p>Textural food ingredients market can be broadly categorized into hydrocolloids, starch and derivatives, emulsifiers, and others. Hydrocolloids are substances that increase viscosity and stabilize mixtures. Starch and its derivatives are commonly used as thickeners and stabilizers in various food products. Emulsifiers help mix ingredients that would normally separate. The "others" category includes miscellaneous texturizing agents such as gelatin, agar, and pectin. These ingredients play a crucial role in enhancing the texture and mouthfeel of food products.</p></p>
