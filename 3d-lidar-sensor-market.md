<p><p>Solid State LiDAR sensors are compact and more reliable due to their solid-state design, offering high resolution and accuracy for various applications. On the other hand, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR sensors use moving parts to scan the environment, providing a wider field of view but also consuming more power and being less durable. Both types cater to different market needs, with solid-state LiDAR being preferred for its compact size and reliability, while mechanical/scanning LiDAR is chosen for its wider scanning capabilities despite its drawbacks.</p></p>
