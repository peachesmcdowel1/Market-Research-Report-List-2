<p><p>Digital repeaters are available in different market types, including Quad Band, Tri Band, Dual Band, and Single Band. Quad Band repeaters support all four major cellular bands, providing coverage for a wide range of frequencies. Tri Band repeaters support three bands, offering coverage for multiple frequencies. Dual Band repeaters support two bands, while Single Band repeaters focus on only one band. Each type of repeater caters to specific frequency needs, ensuring better signal strength and coverage for various applications.</p></p>
