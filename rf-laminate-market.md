<p><ul><li>Under 3 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>3 to 4 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>4 to 5 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li><li>Greater than 5 Dk (Dielectric Constant)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF laminate market types are categorized based on dielectric constant (Dk) values. Under 3 Dk is ideal for high-frequency applications requiring minimal signal loss. 3 to 4 Dk offers a balance between performance and cost for various RF applications. 4 to 5 Dk is suitable for applications needing moderate signal loss. Greater than 5 Dk is used in applications requiring high signal loss. Each market type caters to specific needs for different RF applications based on their dielectric constant values.</p></p>
