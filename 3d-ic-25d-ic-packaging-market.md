<p><strong>The 3D IC & 2.5D IC Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3D TSV</li><li>2.5D and 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D IC packaging involves stacking multiple layers of integrated circuits on top of each other using through-silicon vias (TSVs) for connections. 2.5D IC packaging connects separate dies on a single carrier using interposers. 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP) connects individual dies to a whole wafer before dicing. These different types of packaging technologies provide increased performance, reduced power consumption, and smaller form factors for advanced electronic devices. They are widely used in various applications such as smartphones, tablets, and high-performance computing.</p></p>
