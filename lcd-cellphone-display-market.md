<p><p>LCD Cellphone Display market is segmented into various types such as IPS (In-Plane Switching), ASV (Advanced Super View), TFT (Thin Film Transistor), STN (Super Twisted Nematic), and CSTN (Color Super Twisted Nematic). IPS provides wide viewing angles, ASV offers high contrast ratio and brightness, TFT ensures fast response times, STN is cost-effective with monochrome displays, and CSTN provides color displays at a lower cost. Each market type caters to different consumer needs and preferences in terms of display quality and price.</p></p>
