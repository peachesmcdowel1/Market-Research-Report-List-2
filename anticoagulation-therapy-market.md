<p><p>Anticoagulation therapy market includes two main types of medications- Novel Oral Anticoagulants (NOACs) and Warfarin. NOACs are newer blood thinners that work by targeting specific clotting factors, offering convenient dosing and fewer interactions with food and other medications. Warfarin, on the other hand, is a traditional anticoagulant that requires regular monitoring of blood levels and dietary restrictions. Both types are used to prevent blood clots and reduce the risk of strokes in patients with various medical conditions, with NOACs gaining popularity due to their ease of use.</p></p>
