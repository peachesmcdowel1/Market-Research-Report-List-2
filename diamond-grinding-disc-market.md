<p><strong>The Diamond Grinding Disc Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resinoid Bond Grinding Wheels</li><li>Vitrified Bond Grinding Wheels</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diamond grinding discs are mainly categorized into three types based on the bonding agent used: resinoid bond grinding wheels, vitrified bond grinding wheels, and other types of discs. Resinoid bond grinding wheels are known for their flexibility and shock absorption capabilities, making them ideal for precision grinding tasks. Vitrified bond grinding wheels are recognized for their strong bonding strength and resistance to heat and wear, suited for heavy-duty grinding applications. The "other" category includes diamond grinding discs with unique bonding agents or specialized features catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
