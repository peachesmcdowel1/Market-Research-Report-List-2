<p><p>Scanning lenses can be categorized into different market types based on their design and functionality. One popular type is the Telecentric type, which is known for maintaining a constant magnification regardless of the object distance. This makes them ideal for precision measurement applications. Other types of scanning lenses include non-telecentric lenses, which are more common and versatile in various imaging systems. These lenses offer different characteristics such as larger field of view or smaller size, catering to a wider range of applications.</p></p>
