<p><p>Diabetes treatment market includes three main types of products: Insulin, Oral Hypoglycaemic Drugs, and Non-Insulin Injectable Drugs. Insulin is a hormone that helps regulate blood sugar levels and is essential for managing type 1 diabetes. Oral hypoglycemic drugs are taken orally to lower blood sugar levels in type 2 diabetes patients. Non-insulin injectable drugs are used when oral medications are not effective enough to control blood sugar levels. These three types of treatments play a crucial role in managing diabetes and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
