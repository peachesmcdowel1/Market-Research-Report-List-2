<p><p>Active Matrix Liquid Crystal Display (AMLCD) technology is divided into two main types based on the type of thin film transistor (TFT) used in the display- Polycrystalline Silicon (poly-Si) and Amorphous Silicon (a-Si). Poly-Si displays offer faster response times and better picture quality, making them popular for high-end applications such as smartphones and tablets. On the other hand, a-Si displays are cost effective and widely used in lower-end consumer electronics like budget smartphones and televisions. Both technologies have their own advantages and are widely used in the AMLCD market.</p></p>
