<p><p>Acne treatment markets can be categorized into two main types: inflammatory acne and non-inflammatory acne. Inflammatory acne consists of red, swollen breakouts that are more likely to cause scarring. Non-inflammatory acne, on the other hand, includes blackheads and whiteheads that are milder and less likely to scar. Each type requires different treatment approaches, with inflammatory acne often necessitating stronger medications and non-inflammatory acne typically responding well to topical treatments. Understanding the distinctions between these two types helps to inform the appropriate course of action for effective acne management.</p></p>
