<p><p>Preserved green wall systems utilize real plants that have been preserved to maintain their natural appearance and require minimal maintenance. These systems are popular for their sustainability and aesthetic appeal. On the other hand, synthetic green wall systems consist of fake plants made from materials like plastic or fabric. Both types of green wall systems offer a convenient way to bring greenery into indoor spaces without the need for natural light or watering.</p></p>
