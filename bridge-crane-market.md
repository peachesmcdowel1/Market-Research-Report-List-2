<p><p>A bridge crane, also known as an overhead crane, is a type of crane that consists of parallel runways with a traveling bridge spanning the gap. It is commonly used in industrial settings for lifting and moving heavy loads in a safe and efficient manner. Bridge cranes are equipped with hoists that can move vertically as well as horizontally along the bridge, allowing for precise positioning of the load.</p><p>The Bridge Crane Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for material handling equipment in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and logistics. The adoption of automation and smart technologies in crane systems is also driving the market growth, as companies seek to improve productivity and safety in their operations.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Bridge Crane Market is the integration of IoT and AI technologies in crane systems, enabling real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance capabilities.
