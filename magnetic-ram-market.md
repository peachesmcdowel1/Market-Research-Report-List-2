<p><p>Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM are two types of Magnetic RAM (MRAM) technologies that are gaining traction in the market due to their non-volatile nature and low power consumption. Toggle MRAM uses a dual-terminal magnetic tunnel junction, while STT-MRAM employs spin-transfer torque to read and write data. Both technologies offer fast read and write speeds, high endurance, and low susceptibility to data loss. These MRAM types are expected to find applications in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and enterprise storage.</p></p>
