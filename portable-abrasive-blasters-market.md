<p><p>Portable abrasive blasters are commonly found in two main types: pressure blasters and siphon blasters. Pressure blasters use pressurized air to propel abrasive materials onto a surface, offering higher efficiency and faster results. On the other hand, siphon blasters operate by creating a vacuum that pulls abrasive materials into the airstream. While pressure blasters are preferred for heavy-duty applications and larger surface areas, siphon blasters are more suitable for smaller projects and intricate detailing work. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific needs of the job at hand.</p></p>
