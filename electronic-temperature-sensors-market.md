<p><ul><li>Wired Electronic Temperature Sensors</li><li>Wireless Electronic Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic temperature sensors are classified into two types: wired and wireless sensors. Wired electronic temperature sensors are connected to a central monitoring system through physical cables, providing real-time temperature data. On the other hand, wireless electronic temperature sensors use radio frequency or Bluetooth technology to transmit temperature information wirelessly, eliminating the need for physical connections. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries such as healthcare, automotive, and manufacturing to monitor and regulate temperature levels effectively.</p></p>
