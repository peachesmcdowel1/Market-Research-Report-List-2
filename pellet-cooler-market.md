<p><p>Pellet Coolers are essential equipment used to reduce the temperature of pellets after they have been processed. There are three main types of pellet coolers in the market: Horizontal Type, Vertical Type, and Counterflow Type. Horizontal coolers are preferred for their efficient cooling and compact design. Vertical coolers are known for their space-saving design and easy maintenance. Counterflow coolers provide the highest efficiency in cooling due to the opposite airflow direction of the pellets and cooling air. Each type offers distinct advantages for different production needs.</p></p>
