<p><p>Hand-held acoustic cameras are used for sound imaging and source localization. There are two main types of microphone technologies used in these cameras: MEMS microphones and traditional array microphones.</p><p>MEMS microphones are smaller and more energy-efficient than traditional array microphones, making them ideal for compact and portable acoustic cameras. Traditional array microphones, on the other hand, offer higher resolution and accuracy but are bulkier and consume more power. Both technologies cater to different market needs, with MEMS microphones being more suitable for consumer applications and traditional array microphones for professional use.</p></p>
