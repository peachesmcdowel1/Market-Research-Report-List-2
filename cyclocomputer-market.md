<p><p>The cyclocomputer market can be divided into three main types: wired computer, wireless computer, and wireless & GPS computer. Wired computers use physical wires to connect the sensors to the display unit. Wireless computers use radio signals to transmit data from the sensors to the display. Wireless & GPS computers combine the functionality of wireless connectivity with GPS technology for more accurate tracking of speed, distance, and location. Each type offers different levels of convenience and accuracy for cyclists looking to track their performance.</p></p>
