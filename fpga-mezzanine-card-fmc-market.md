<p><p>FPGA Mezzanine Card (FMC) comes in two main market types: Low Pin Count (LPC) and High Pin Count (HPC). LPC FMCs have a smaller number of pins, making them suitable for applications with limited space and lower-cost requirements. On the other hand, HPC FMCs have a higher pin count, allowing for more connectivity options and higher performance capabilities. Both LPC and HPC FMCs cater to different customer needs, providing flexibility in choosing the right card for specific applications.</p></p>
