<p><p>Trimming presses are classified into two main types based on their movement capabilities. Active trimming presses are mobile and can be easily moved around the production floor to perform trimming tasks at different locations. On the other hand, stationary trimming presses are fixed in one position and are typically used for high-volume production tasks where the workpiece needs to be brought to the machine. Both types of trimming presses offer unique advantages and are suited for different applications depending on the production requirements.</p></p>
