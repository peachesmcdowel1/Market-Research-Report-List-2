<p><p>A Pulse Oximetry Probe is a medical device used to measure the oxygen saturation level in a patient's blood. It works by emitting light from two different sources through a translucent part of the patient's body, typically a fingertip or earlobe. The device then measures the amount of light that is absorbed by oxygenated and deoxygenated blood, providing a reading of the patient's oxygen saturation level.</p><p>The Pulse Oximetry Probe Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period.
