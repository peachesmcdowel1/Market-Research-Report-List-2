<p><p>Air Oil Heat Exchangers are available in two main types - with fan and without fan. Air Oil Heat Exchangers with fan are equipped with a fan that helps in circulating air through the heat exchanger, providing efficient cooling. On the other hand, Air Oil Heat Exchangers without fan rely on natural convection for air circulation. Both types are used in various applications where cooling of oil is required, but the choice between the two depends on factors such as space constraints, energy efficiency, and cooling requirements.</p></p>
