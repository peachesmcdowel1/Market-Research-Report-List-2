<p><p>The 4K Endoscopic Camera System market offers two main types of camera systems: White Light Camera System and Fluorescence Camera System. The White Light Camera System provides high-resolution images using white light sources, making it ideal for general endoscopic procedures. On the other hand, the Fluorescence Camera System enhances visualization by using fluorescent dyes that emit light when exposed to specific wavelengths, allowing for better detection of certain tissues or abnormalities. Both systems cater to different needs within the medical field, providing advanced imaging capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
