<p><p>Next-generation firewalls come in three main types - hardware, virtual, and cloud. Hardware-based firewalls consist of physical devices that are installed within a network for on-premise protection. Virtual firewalls are software-based solutions that can be deployed on virtual machines or servers, providing flexibility and scalability. Cloud-based firewalls are hosted and managed off-site, offering remote protection for cloud-based applications and data. Each type of firewall offers unique benefits and features, catering to different network environments and security needs.</p></p>
