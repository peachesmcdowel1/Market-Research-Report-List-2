<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point of Entry (POE)</li><li>Point of Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential water treatment market can be categorized into Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU) systems. POE systems treat water at the entry point of the house, ensuring that all water used for cooking, bathing, and laundry is clean and safe. POU systems, on the other hand, treat water at the specific point where it is being used, such as the kitchen faucet or showerhead. Both types of systems aim to provide households with clean and safe drinking water.</p></p>
