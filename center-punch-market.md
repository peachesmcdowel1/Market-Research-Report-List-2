<p><p>Center punches are versatile tools used for making precise markings before drilling or driving screws. There are different market types of center punches, including pneumatic and automatic options. Pneumatic center punches are powered by compressed air, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications and ensuring consistent force with each use. Automatic center punches feature a spring-loaded mechanism that automatically strikes when pressed against a surface, making them convenient and efficient for quick jobs. Both types cater to different needs in the market for precision marking tasks.</p></p>
