<p><p>Linear drainage systems are common solutions for managing surface water runoff in both residential and commercial settings. Surface drainage systems include channels and grates that collect water from the surface and direct it to a designated outlet. Underground drainage systems, on the other hand, consist of pipes buried beneath the surface to collect and transport water away from the area. Both types of systems are crucial in preventing flooding and erosion, providing effective water management solutions for various environments.</p></p>
