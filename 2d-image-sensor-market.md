<p><strong>The 2D Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 2D Image Sensor market is dominated by three types: CMOS, CCD, and Others. CMOS sensors are widely used due to their lower power consumption, faster readout speeds, and cost-effectiveness. CCD sensors, on the other hand, offer higher image quality and better performance in low-light conditions, making them popular in industries such as healthcare and security. The Others category includes emerging technologies like back-illuminated sensors and stacked CMOS sensors, which are gaining traction for their improved sensitivity and image quality.</p></p>
