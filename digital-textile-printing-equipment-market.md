<p><p>Digital textile printing equipment market includes two main types: Direct to Fabric printing and Direct to Garment printing. Direct to Fabric printing involves printing directly onto the fabric, allowing for intricate designs and vibrant colors. This method is commonly used for bulk fabric production for items such as curtains, upholstery, and apparel. On the other hand, Direct to Garment printing involves printing directly onto finished garments, allowing for personalized designs and shorter production runs. This method is often used for custom apparel and promotional merchandise.</p></p>
