<p><p>Flight tracking market types include ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast), FANS (Future Air Navigation System), and PFTS (Precision Flight Tracking System). ADS-B relies on aircraft broadcasting their position and other information to ground stations and other aircraft. FANS incorporates satellite technology to aid in aircraft communication and navigation. PFTS uses advanced technology to track flights with precision and accuracy. These different market types cater to the diverse needs and requirements of the aviation industry, ensuring efficient and safe flight operations.</p></p>
