<p><p>The Marine Deep Cycle Battery market offers two main types: Flooded Lead Acid Batteries and Lithium Ion Batteries. Flooded Lead Acid Batteries are the traditional option, known for their reliability and affordability. They require regular maintenance such as topping up with distilled water. In contrast, Lithium Ion Batteries are a newer and more advanced option, offering higher energy density, longer lifespan, and no maintenance requirements. While they tend to be more expensive upfront, they provide superior performance and convenience in the long run.</p></p>
