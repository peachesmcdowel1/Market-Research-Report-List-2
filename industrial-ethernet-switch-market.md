<p><ul><li>Modular switches</li><li>Fixed configuration switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Ethernet Switch market consists of two main types: Modular switches and Fixed configuration switches. Modular switches allow for the addition or removal of modules to customize the switch according to specific requirements. On the other hand, Fixed configuration switches have a predetermined number of ports and cannot be expanded. Modular switches offer greater flexibility and scalability, while fixed configuration switches are simpler and more cost-effective for smaller setups. Both types cater to different industrial networking needs.</p></p>
