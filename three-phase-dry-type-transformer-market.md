<p><strong>The Three Phase Dry Type Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Winding Transformer</li><li>Three Winding Transformer</li><li>Self-Couple Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three-phase dry-type transformers are categorized into three main types based on their winding configurations: Double Winding Transformer, Three Winding Transformer, and Self-Coupled Transformer. Double Winding Transformers have two separate windings for primary and secondary circuits. Three Winding Transformers have an additional winding for special applications like grounding or regulating systems. Self-Coupled Transformers combine primary and secondary windings into a single coil, reducing size and cost. Each type serves specific purposes and offers different advantages in terms of efficiency, voltage regulation, and space requirements.</p></p>
