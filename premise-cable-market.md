<p><p>Premise cable market consists of two main types: Copper cable and Fiber Optic cable. Copper cables are traditional and widely used for their ability to carry electrical signals efficiently. They are generally less expensive than fiber optic cables, but have limitations in terms of bandwidth and distance. On the other hand, Fiber optic cables use light pulses to transmit data, offering higher bandwidth and faster speeds over longer distances. They are becoming increasingly popular for high-speed internet and data transmission in modern networks.</p></p>
