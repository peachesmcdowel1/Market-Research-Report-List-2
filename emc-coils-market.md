<p><p>EMC coils are designed to minimize electromagnetic interference in electronic devices. Two main types of EMC coils are AC EMC coils and DC EMC coils. AC EMC coils are used to suppress interference in alternating current circuits, while DC EMC coils are used in direct current circuits. Both types of coils are essential for ensuring the smooth operation and reliability of electronic equipment by filtering out unwanted electromagnetic noise and signals. The market for EMC coils continues to grow as the demand for interference-free devices increases in various industries.</p></p>
