<p><p>Zonal isolation packers are used in the oil and gas industry to provide a barrier between different zones in a well. There are two main types of zonal isolation packers - permanent packers and retrievable packers. Permanent packers are installed in the wellbore and remain in place for the life of the well. Retrievable packers can be easily removed and reinstalled as needed. Both types of packers play a crucial role in ensuring well integrity and maximizing operational efficiency.</p></p>
