<p><p>3D Radar systems are categorized into three main types based on their operational range- Long Range, Medium Range, and Short Range. Long Range radars are designed to detect targets over a wide area, typically up to several hundred kilometers away. Medium Range radar systems cover a smaller area but provide more detailed information about targets within their range, usually up to a distance of about 100 kilometers. Short Range radars are used for close-range surveillance, typically up to a distance of 10 kilometers.</p></p>
