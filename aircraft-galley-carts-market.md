Cabinet type galley carts have enclosed storage space with doors that open outward, providing easy access to food and beverage items. On the other hand, frame type galley carts feature an open frame design with shelves for storing items. Each type appeals to different airline companies based on their specific needs and preferences, offering flexibility in organizing and serving inflight meals and beverages.</p></p>
