<p><p>Medium voltage plastic film capacitors are commonly used for power factor correction and filtering applications in various industries. Two main types of medium voltage plastic film capacitors are polyester film capacitors and metallized film capacitors. Polyester film capacitors have a dielectric made of polyester film and are commonly used in applications where high insulation resistance and stability are required. Metallized film capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the film dielectric, providing improved self-healing properties and high energy density for applications requiring high reliability and long lifespan.</p></p>
