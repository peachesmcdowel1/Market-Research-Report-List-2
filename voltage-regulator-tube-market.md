<p><strong>The Voltage-regulator Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroresonant</li><li>Tap Switching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage-regulator tube market consists of various types, including Ferroresonant and Tap Switching regulators. Ferroresonant regulators use a combination of a saturated reactor and capacitor to maintain a constant output voltage, making them suitable for applications requiring high reliability. On the other hand, Tap Switching regulators utilize multiple taps on an autotransformer to adjust the output voltage, offering flexibility and efficiency. Both types cater to different needs in the market, providing options for regulating voltage in various electrical systems.</p></p>
