<p><p>Automotive Injector Nozzle Market consists of different types including Single-point Fuel Injection (SFI), Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), SFI+GDI, and Diesel Direct Injection (DDI). SFI injects fuel into the intake manifold near the intake valve, GDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, SFI+GDI combines both types for improved fuel efficiency and performance, while DDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber of diesel engines. Each type offers its own advantages in terms of fuel efficiency, power output, and emissions control.</p></p>
