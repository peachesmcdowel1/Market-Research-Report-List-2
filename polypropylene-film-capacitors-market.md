<p><strong>The Polypropylene Film Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li><li>Metalized Polypropylene Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene film capacitors are categorized into two main types: plain polypropylene film capacitors and metalized polypropylene film capacitors. Plain polypropylene film capacitors are made of plain polypropylene film without any additional metal layers, offering stable performance and high insulation resistance. On the other hand, metalized polypropylene film capacitors have a thin metal layer deposited on the polypropylene film, providing improved conductivity, reliability, and self-healing properties. These capacitors are widely used in various electronic applications for energy storage and signal coupling.</p></p>
