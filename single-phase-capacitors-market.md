<p><strong>The Single-phase Capacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-phase capacitors can be categorized into two main types: radial and axial capacitors. Radial capacitors have leads that are connected to the body of the capacitor at opposite ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting on circuit boards. Axial capacitors, on the other hand, have leads attached to the body at the same end, making them ideal for applications where space is limited and where a longer component is required. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in various electrical and electronic applications.</p></p>
