<p><p>Aboveground fuel tanks come in two main types: single wall and double wall. Single wall tanks consist of one layer of material and are more prone to leakage and environmental hazards. On the other hand, double wall tanks have an additional layer of protection that helps prevent leaks and provide greater safety. Double wall tanks are preferred in industries where the risk of fuel spills or leaks is higher, such as gas stations or industrial facilities.</p></p>
