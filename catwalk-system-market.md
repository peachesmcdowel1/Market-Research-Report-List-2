<p><p>Catwalk system market includes two main types: Flat Catwalk System and Rigged Catwalk System. Flat Catwalk System involves a flat surface elevated above the ground that provides a safe and sturdy platform for walking and working at heights. On the other hand, Rigged Catwalk System consists of suspended walkways that are supported by cables or other structural elements. Both types of catwalk systems are commonly used in industries such as construction, entertainment, and manufacturing to provide access to hard-to-reach areas and ensure the safety of workers.</p></p>
