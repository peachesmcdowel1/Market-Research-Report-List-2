<p><p>Single Entry Racks are designed for warehouses with space constraints where forklifts can only enter from one side to load and unload pallets. This type of rack maximizes storage capacity by utilizing floor space efficiently. On the other hand, Double Entry Racks allow forklifts to access pallets from both sides, increasing operational efficiency by reducing the time required to move goods in and out. This type of rack is ideal for high-traffic warehouses where quick and easy access to inventory is essential.</p></p>
