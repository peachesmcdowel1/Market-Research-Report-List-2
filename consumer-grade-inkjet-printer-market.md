<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Thermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer grade inkjet printers can be divided into two main types based on their printing technology: piezoelectric type and thermal type. Piezoelectric inkjet printers feature tiny electrically charged crystals that create small droplets of ink, resulting in higher precision and color accuracy. On the other hand, thermal inkjet printers use heat to eject ink droplets onto the paper. While thermal printers are generally faster and more cost-effective, piezoelectric printers are known for their superior print quality and longevity.</p></p>
