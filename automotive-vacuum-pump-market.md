<p><p>Automotive vacuum pumps can be classified into two main types: electric vacuum pumps and mechanical vacuum pumps. Electric vacuum pumps are powered by electricity and are commonly used in modern vehicles for applications such as brake boosters and emissions control systems. Mechanical vacuum pumps, on the other hand, are powered by the engine's mechanical motion and are often found in older vehicles. Both types of vacuum pumps play a crucial role in maintaining proper vacuum levels in various automotive systems, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.</p></p>
