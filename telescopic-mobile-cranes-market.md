<p><p>Telescopic mobile cranes are classified into different types based on their jib configurations. Folding jib cranes have a jib that can be folded for easy transportation and storage. Fixed jib cranes have a stationary jib that cannot be adjusted. Luffing jib cranes have a jib that can move vertically to work in tight spaces. Other types of telescopic mobile cranes may include various combinations of jib configurations to meet specific lifting requirements. Each type offers unique advantages for different job applications.</p></p>
