<p><p>Surgical Instrument Table market is segmented into Activity Type and Stationary Type. Activity Type surgical tables are designed for procedures that require constant movement and adjustment during surgery. These tables often have multiple adjustable sections and specialized features for different surgical specialties. On the other hand, Stationary Type tables are fixed in place and do not offer the same level of versatility as Activity Type tables. They are commonly used for less complex procedures where limited movement is required.</p></p>
