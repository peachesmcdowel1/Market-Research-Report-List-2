<p><p>The fuses for electrical equipment and electronic products market can be categorized into four main types: filamentous, flake, glass tube, and ceramic tube. Filamentous fuses are made of a fine wire enclosed in a protective casing. Flake fuses consist of a small semiconductor or metal flake within a plastic housing. Glass tube fuses are transparent tubes containing a conducting metal wire. Ceramic tube fuses are made of a durable ceramic material. Each type of fuse serves to protect electronic devices from overcurrent and short circuits.</p></p>
