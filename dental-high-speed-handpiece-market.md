<p><strong>The Dental High Speed Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust Handpiece</li><li>Outside Exhaust Handpiece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental high-speed handpieces are categorized into two main types: Inside Exhaust Handpieces and Outside Exhaust Handpieces. Inside Exhaust Handpieces have the exhaust air vent inside the handpiece, which reduces aerosols and provides a cleaner working environment. Outside Exhaust Handpieces have the exhaust air vent located outside the handpiece, allowing for easier access and maintenance. Both types offer different advantages and cater to various preferences among dental professionals. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as infection control protocols and personal preferences.</p></p>
